üéØ What We Do

Real Adventures, Real Skills, Real Character

Our troop has built an impressive campsite featuring a commanding 30-foot flagpole where Old Glory flies high. We've conducted solemn flag retirement ceremonies honoring our nation's colors, built and launched model rockets skyward, and conquered trails at both Ricketts Glen and Worlds End State Parks.

Around crackling campfires and with cast iron Dutch ovens, we master the art of outdoor cooking. We've constructed a working rope-making machine and are learning essential knots and ropework. At our dedicated campsite, boys test their aim at our archery and BB rifle range, practice hatchet throwing, and engage in pugil stick combat training. Through hands-on first aid instruction and biblical character training, we forge the next generation of American patriots and Christian warriors.
