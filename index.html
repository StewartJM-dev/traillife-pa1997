Welcome to Troop PA-1997

We are Trail Life USA Troop PA-1997, chartered in July 2024 and proudly sponsored by Living Word Baptist Church in Sweet Valley, Pennsylvania. What began as a dream and a prayer in December 2023 has grown into a thriving brotherhood of young men learning to "Walk Worthy" through outdoor adventure, character development, and biblical discipleship. Our troop serves the rural communities of northeastern Pennsylvania, where the mountains, forests, and streams of God's creation become our classroom. From our dedicated campsite on church property to the trails of Ricketts Glen and Worlds End State Parks, we're building the next generation of godly men who honor God, lead with integrity, serve others, and embrace adventure. Whether learning to build a rope-making machine, raising the American flag on a 30-foot flagpole our Trailmen built themselves, or conquering challenging hiking trails, every activity is designed to forge character alongside practical skills. We believe that boys need a place to be boys and a clear path to become men‚Äîmen of courage, conviction, and unwavering faith. From our founding members who took the first brave steps to the families who continue to join our mission, Trail Life PA-1997 is more than a troop‚Äîwe're a family united by shared values, outdoor adventure, and the commitment to raise young men who will impact their communities and honor Christ in everything they do. Welcome to our troop. Welcome to the adventure. Welcome to a brotherhood that will last a lifetime.

Our Mission

To guide generations of courageous young men to honor God, lead with integrity, serve others, and experience outdoor adventure.
